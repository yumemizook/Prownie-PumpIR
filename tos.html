1. Age Requirement

  • You must be at least 13 years old to use this website.
  • We reserve the right to ban or restrict access to users under 13, with or without notice, to protect our community.
  • If you registered before turning 13 and were banned, you may contact an admin for reinstatement once you are over 13.

2. Account Management

  • Each player is allowed one account only. Creating or using multiple accounts (except in special, admin-approved cases) is prohibited and may result in a ban.
  • Sharing an account between multiple players is not allowed. Accounts found to be shared or used for "buffing" will be banned.
  • You may request account deletion via Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This will remove your account and all associated scores.
  • After deleting your account, you will not be able to register again for a very long time. Please consider carefully before deleting.
  • Creating a new account after deletion is considered multi-accounting and will be treated as such.

3. Score Submission

  • All submitted scores must be legitimate and accurate.

4. Reporting & Abuse

  • If you notice an erroneous or suspicious score, use the Report Score feature or report the user via their profile.
  • Reports should be made in good faith. Abusing the reporting system (e.g., false or spam reports) may result in temporary or permanent restrictions.

6. Prohibited Behaviors

Examples of prohibited behaviors include, but are not limited to:

  • Harassing or threatening other users or admins
  • Vandalizing message boards

  • Submitting fake or fraudulent scores
  • Engaging in hate speech, racism, or discrimination

7. Enforcement & Appeals

  • Violations of these terms may result in temporary or permanent bans, depending on severity.
  • To appeal a ban, contact an admin and provide your case. Appeals may be denied if your offense history is severe.

8. Questions

If you have any questions or concerns about these terms, please contact an admin.
