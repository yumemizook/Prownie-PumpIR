Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Pump It Up score tracking platform

About the Platform

This is a dedicated score tracking website for Pump It Up home users, designed to provide an alternative to the official arcade-focused piugame.com platform.

Why not use piugame.com?

While piugame.com exists, it's primarily designed for arcade users connected to the Andamiro network. Our platform serves home users who don't have access to arcade cabinets.

Score Submission

Submit your scores through the score page by clicking the "Upload Score" button. Our system automatically calculates scores using the Phoenix scoring system for consistency.

Why Phoenix scoring instead of combo-based?

We use the Phoenix scoring system (capped at 1,000,000 points) because combo-based scoring is outdated and requires complex input data. Phoenix scoring makes it easier to compare scores and understand performance.

Pumpbility System

Pumpbility measures your skill level based on your grade and chart difficulty. View detailed calculations in Mr.Weq's comprehensive spreadsheet.

Your Pumpbility is displayed on your profile page and we're continuously improving the calculation accuracy.

Score Verification & Reporting

We're committed to maintaining fair leaderboards. If you encounter suspicious scores:

  • Report them through the score page's "Report Score" feature
  • Our moderation team will review and remove illegitimate scores
  • Cheating results in leaderboard exclusion and/or account bans
Note: Ridiculous scores on extremely difficult charts (like a Perfect Game on 1948 D32) are prioritized for review.

Account Management

Profile Updates

Visit your profile page to update your data. If scores aren't updating, they may be pending verification - contact an admin for assistance.

Username & Profile Changes

Edit your profile through Settings > Edit Profile. Username changes have a 7-day cooldown, but other details can be modified freely.

Account Deactivation

Currently, we only offer account deletion. Contact us to temporarily lock your account if you need a break.

Account Deletion

Delete your account through Settings > Delete Account. This action is permanent and prevents re-registration for an extended period. (At least 1 year)

Community Guidelines

Prohibited Behavior

  • Hate speech, racism, or discrimination
  • Harassment of other users
  • Inappropriate profile content
  • Multi-accounting

Reporting Users

Report violations through the user's profile page > Report User. Our moderation team will investigate and take appropriate action.

Our Motto: "Trust your fellow users" - We believe in our community but enforce rules to maintain a positive environment.

Multi-Accounting Policy

We strictly prohibit creating multiple accounts. This policy ensures fair leaderboards and prevents abuse.

Important: Creating an account after deleting a previous one counts as multi-accounting and results in a ban.

Multi-accounting creates unfair advantages and clogs our system. We actively detect and ban violators, including IP blocks for persistent offenders.

For more information on why multi-accounting is harmful, see these resources:

  • Reddit Discussion on Multi-Accounting
  • Osu! Forum Discussion
  • Osu! Wiki on Account Restrictions

Enforcement & Appeals

Punishment System

  • Minor offenses: Warning
  • Major offenses: Leaderboard exclusion and score removal
  • Severe offenses: Site ban

Ban Appeals

If you believe you were banned unfairly, contact an admin to appeal. During appeals, do not create alternative accounts - this will result in additional bans.

Warning: Bypassing bans through multiple accounts leads to IP blocks and potentially ISP-level restrictions and/or country-wide restrictions.

Technical Support

Data Issues

If your data appears corrupted or randomly changes, contact an admin immediately. We maintain backups but can only restore data within a limited timeframe.

Score Update Problems

Unverified scores won't appear in leaderboards. Contact us if you notice delays in score verification.

We welcome suggestions for improving the platform. Contact us through the contact page to share your ideas.

Admin Contact

Need help? Find admin contact information in the page footer. Our moderation team is currently very small but is soon to be expanded - we handle reports on a first-come, first-served basis.
